Kusunoki Midori

Her once-respected parents are proud of being wealthy.
However, in her current class she is being shunned and alienated; receiving the same opposite treatment as before.

In reality, she does not long to be a fleeting princess.
Today however, by the Tea-Gentleman's power, she has been able to play the part of an incorporeal one.

Incidentally, she has considerable acting ability. If her current misfortune was endured, then she would surely flourish.

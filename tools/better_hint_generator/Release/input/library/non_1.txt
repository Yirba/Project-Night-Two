Nonomiya Takeshi

A certain falsely-accused boy obsessed with uncovering the truth with photographs.
He follows Numata Yoko and repeatedly harasses him with spy photos he takes.

However, the subject has taken notice of his techniques.
He considers his mission probably that of a detective, investigator, or paparazzi.

He likes birds, and his future dream as a professional photographer is to take photos of them.
